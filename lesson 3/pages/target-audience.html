TARGET AUDIENCE

The Target Audience for this website will be:

  • Who: residents of southeastern Idaho in the cities of Preston, Soda Springs, and Fish Haven.
  • Age: users will vary from 12 to 100 years, but the emphasis will be on the 18 to 50 year old age range
  • Technology: Basic understanding of consulting a webpage
  • Income: Median income levels will be typically around $50,000 annually
  • Motivation: Inform residents of current weather conditions in their area
