CONTRAST

Spigot Design

spigotdesign.com

Contrast is particularly evident when you squint at this image. The dark purple background makes the company's mark and the text pop off the page because of the contrast in the values. Then the eye is drawn to the lighter values in the header showcasing the logo and menu.

REPETITION

Apple

apple.com

The repetition of same size images in various colors keeps the user's eye engaged as it moves back and forth between the app images. The mind understands that these items work together to make a whole and is continuously drawn back down to the most important app image on the page for Apple's App Store.

VISUAL HIERARCHY

Banana Republic

bananarepublic.gap.com

This site uses Visual Hierarchy to help the user navigate around the page. 1) the large photos of humans - the eyes being the focal point, 2) large white text visually grouped together with buttons, 3) yellow draws the eye to the banner, 4) white banner, 5) header including menu, logo and tabs, and 6) footer with sale information.
