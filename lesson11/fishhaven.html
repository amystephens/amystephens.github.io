Bear Lake Monster Winterfest

by Summer Wheatley

Fish Haven residents and people from all across the country are looking forward to the annual Bear Lake Monster Winterfest January 28-30, 2021.

On Thursday evening, the Monster Winterfest Social will include free family-friendly games for all ages at the Sunrise Resort and Event Center from 4-10pm. Events include a movie, trivia, karaoke, auction, hot chocolate and donuts. The Monster Outdoor Expo will also being going on at this time at the Sunrise Resort. Check out the vendors and pick up your own pet polar bear at the adoption center.

Friday morning the Monster 5K Run/Walk will kick off at at 10 am. Participants need to pick up their packets from 8:30-9:30 am. Athletes arrive in full costumes to hide their identities because only crazy people would want to run here in January.

Saturday at 11 am is the Monster Plunge. Join us (or sign up someone you don't like) to go jump in a lake. Once again, costumes are encouraged because, well, same reason as before you crazy people. This is event is sure to take your breath away and leave you shaking.

Saturday night from 6-10 pm will be the Monster After Party at the Sunrise Resort and Event Center. At 6 pm, an award ceremony will be held to honor any survivors from the Monster Winterfest competitions. Music and dancing will run from 7-10 pm. Enjoy fireworks at dusk - because apparently standing outside in freezing cold weather is fun for some people.
