Festival of Lights 2020

by Summer Wheatley

The City of Preston is excited to host its annual Festival of Lights again this holiday season on Saturday, November 24. We will kick off the festivities at 12:30 pm with the Live Entertainment/Variety Show at the Preston High Auditorium featuring a guest performance from our local movie star, Napolean Dynamite. We are still accepting performers. Like always, no talent is required; but if you like to be on a stage, contact LaFawnduh Dynamite at Betty's Salon.

Mosey on down to State Street at 3:00 pm for every local's favorite event - The Bed Race. While anyone outside of Preston may wonder what in the world this is or how it possibly started, we know it makes total sense to race beds down the street to get everyone in the Christmas Spirit. For more information, stop on down at Ralphs's and pick up your Bed Race Packet.

Join us at the Worm Creek Opera House just down from the old Johnson place across from the Smiths for a free family-friendly movie at 4:00 pm. Martha is making popcorn and Jane is supplying the homemade rootbeer. Bring a blanket because if you remember from last year, the heat is not always working this time of year.

And last, but not least, will be our fireworks and lighted parade on Saturday, November 24 at 6:30 pm. While some may wonder why anyone in their right mind would think it was a good idea to be outside in the freezing cold at night, we know the joy that comes from being frozen from the waist down. We hope to see you there - unless all of our eyes are frozen.
