Local Outdoor Adventure Spotlights

by Deb Bradshaw

Winter is here and it is already cold outside making it the perfect opportunity to take a short drive to visit Lava Hot Springs and warm up. Their Geothermal Hot Springs range from 102˚ to 112˚ and are a fun way to relax. If you are looking for a deal, Wednesdays are their Family Deal Day. Get a family pass for up to four people for only $18.50. If you are wanting to take friends or family with you, group rates are also available. Advance tickets can be purchased through their website to make it easy to plan ahead.

Lava Hot Springs boasts several different hot pools of varying sizes and temperatures. Courageous soakers will take the opportunity to make snow angels in the subzero temperatures while switching between pools. Nothing is more satisfying than having frozen nose hairs while the rest of your body feels a little on the hot side. If you are lucky enough to live in Idaho, you know that over half of the year is winter making the idea of warming up extra appealing.
